Working out Log

Simply follow along workouts you find on youtube at home. Other than that here are some exercises to do on your own:

  • Resistance band face pull
  • Resistance band face pull with shrug
  • Angled Resistance band low pull
  • Trap Shrugs
  • Kettlebell single leg deadlift
  • Kettlebell Bulgarian Split Squats
  • Under-the-table Australian Pullup
  • Resistance band trap cross
  • One arm kettlebell row (elbow driving a pendulum, arm close to body)
  • Kettlebell shoulder press
  • Forearm kettlebell curl

And the following exercises for the gym:

  • Bench Press
  • Pullups
  • Lat Pulldown
  • Deadlifts
  • FacePulls
  • Seated Rows
  • Cable Flys
  • Leg Press Machine
  • Rowing machine (cardio) - work towards a sub 6 2k

You have to be really efficient with your time if you want to fit all of this in - and you would need to consume 4000-5000 calories a day jut to keep up
